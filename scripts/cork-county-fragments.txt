    <h4>Project Title:&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;R613 Balygarvan Trafic Calming Scheme (TCS)</h4>
    <h5>LOCATION</h5>
    <p>R613 Ballygarvan Traffic Calming Scheme (TCS), Ballygarvan, Co. Cork</p>
    <p>Project Area - Townlands:&nbsp;</p>
    <p>Ballygarvan, Ballyduhig South.</p>
    <h5>NATURE &amp; EXTENT OF DEVELOPMENT</h5>
    <p>An overview of the proposed development includes the following elements:</p>
    <ul><li>Alterations to existing footpaths &amp; construction of new linking footpaths, installation of concrete buildouts.</li>
    <li>Installation of a new raised table and a new controlled pedestrian crossing, speed ramps on R613 on exits from the village.</li>
    <li>Installation of new road markings and signage.</li>
    <li>Alterations to existing storm drainage and construction of a new drainage.</li>
    <li>Alterations to the existing Ballygarvan Bridge including:
    <ul><li>Extension of the bridge to the southern side to accommodate new footpath [extension to bridge of approx 2.0m]</li>
    <li>Construction of new bridge retaining wall approx. 20m in length, to allow for the footpath upgrade on the southern side&nbsp;</li>
    <li>Riverbed, Embankment &amp; Masonry repairs.</li>
    </ul></li>
    <li>Trimming of existing vegetation to improve visibility sightlines &amp; all ancillary works required to deliver the proposed upgrade works.</li>
    <li>Works will be carried out within existing roads extents owned by Cork County Council and lands acquired from private landowners.</li>
